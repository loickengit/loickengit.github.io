Why dieting doesn’t usually work

Our body is like a thermostat, if we try to lose weight, if trys to gain it back;

Like a thermostat, you can try to change the temperature in you house by opening a window in the winter, but that’s not going to change the setting on the thermostat.

So, the right way to do with you diet is:

Eat mindfully

learning to understand your body’s signals

so that you eat when you’re hungry and stop when you’re full
